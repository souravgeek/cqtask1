Alumini Clock Tower

The Alumni Clock Tower at IIT Kharagpur, inaugurated in 2015, is a prominent 60-foot landmark gifted by the Alumni Foundation for the institute's Diamond Jubilee. Located near the Technology Students' Gymkhana, it merges modern design with the institute's heritage, featuring clocks on all four sides. It symbolizes the academic excellence of IIT Kharagpur and serves as a meeting point for students, engraved with names of contributing alumni, embodying their enduring connection to the alma mater..

Main Building, IIT Kharagpur

The Main Building of IIT Kharagpur, often referred to as the 'Institute Building', stands as an iconic structure symbolizing the academic prowess and historical significance of one of India's premier educational institutions. This majestic edifice, with its imposing red brick façade, is a blend of colonial and contemporary architectural styles, reflecting the rich heritage of the institute. Located at the heart of the sprawling campus, it houses key administrative offices, including the Director's office, and several important departments. The building's grand corridors and spacious halls have been witnesses to countless scholarly pursuits and intellectual discussions over the years. Its clock tower, a notable feature, not only adds to the aesthetic appeal but also serves as a symbolic reminder of the institute's timeless pursuit of knowledge and innovation. The Main Building is more than just a structure; it's a beacon of learning and a source of inspiration for generations of students and academicians who have walked its hallowed halls.

Technology Students Gymkhana

The Technology Students' Gymkhana (TSG) at IIT Kharagpur is the hub of extracurricular and co-curricular activities, playing a pivotal role in shaping the all-round development of students. Located centrally in the campus, TSG is a vibrant center of student life, encompassing facilities for sports, cultural activities, and student welfare. It houses various indoor and outdoor sports facilities, including courts for badminton, basketball, and other sports, along with spaces for cultural pursuits like music, dance, and art. TSG is also the administrative body for student clubs and societies, fostering a spirit of creativity, leadership, and teamwork. The Gymkhana organizes major events like the annual cultural fest 'Spring Fest' and the sports meet 'Inter IIT Sports Meet', which are highlights of the campus calendar. This dynamic space is not just about leisure and recreation; it's a crucible where leadership and interpersonal skills are honed, contributing significantly to the holistic development of the students at IIT Kharagpur.
